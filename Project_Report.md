# **PROJECT REPORT 1**

## **Website Audit & Digital Marketing Implementation**

### **Organization:** Unified Mentor

### **Project Type:** Website Audit & Digital Marketing Strategy

### **Domain:** E-Learning

---

### **1. Introduction**

Unified Mentor is an online e-learning platform offering skill-based courses and mentorship programs. The website plays a critical role in attracting leads, building trust, and converting visitors into enrolled learners. However, increasing competition and changing user behavior made it necessary to conduct a detailed website audit and digital marketing analysis to identify performance gaps and growth opportunities.

---

### **2. Objectives**

* To analyze the website’s **on-page, off-page, and technical SEO**
* To identify **user experience (UX) and performance issues**
* To improve **organic visibility and brand awareness**
* To design a **digital marketing strategy** for lead generation and engagement

---

### **3. Methodology**

* **SEO tools used:** Ubersuggest, manual UX testing
* **Audit areas covered:**

  * On-page SEO
  * Technical performance
  * Content quality
  * Mobile & desktop usability
  * Brand awareness analysis

---

### **4. Key Issues Identified**

**SEO Issues**

* Duplicate meta titles and descriptions
* Low word count on important pages
* Missing H1 headings
* Poor URL structure

**Technical Issues**

* Slow mobile load time
* Poor desktop interactivity

**User Experience Issues**

* Confusing desktop layout
* Old and outdated content
* Low brand awareness among students

---

### **5. Solutions Implemented**

* Optimized meta titles, descriptions, and headings
* Improved internal linking and content depth
* Enhanced mobile page speed
* Simplified desktop navigation
* Strengthened social media presence
* Introduced campus ambassador programs

---

### **6. Expected Results**

* Improved search engine rankings
* Better mobile and desktop user experience
* Increased organic traffic
* Higher student engagement and conversions

---

### **7. Conclusion**

The website audit revealed that Unified Mentor has a strong foundation but lacks optimization in key performance areas. By addressing SEO, content, and usability issues, the platform can significantly improve its online visibility, user engagement, and lead conversion potential.

# **PROJECT REPORT 2**

## **Meta (Facebook & Instagram) Ads Campaign**

### **Brand:** TrendHive

### **Project Type:** Brand Awareness Campaign

### **Platform:** Meta Ads (Facebook & Instagram)

---

### **1. Introduction**

TrendHive is an online fashion e-commerce brand targeting Gen Z and young millennials with trendy and affordable clothing. Despite competitive pricing and stylish products, the brand struggled with low awareness and engagement in a highly competitive fashion market.

---

### **2. Problem Statement**

* Low brand visibility
* Limited social media reach
* High competition from Myntra and Ajio
* Low engagement and conversions

---

### **3. Campaign Objectives**

* Increase brand awareness
* Reach new fashion-interested audiences
* Improve engagement and brand recall

---

### **4. Campaign Strategy**

* **Campaign Type:** Brand Awareness
* **Ad Formats:** Image ads, carousel ads, short video/Reels
* **Creative Approach:** Trend-based visuals and lifestyle content

---

### **5. Target Audience**

* Location: India (Tier 1 & Tier 2 cities)
* Age: 18–30 years
* Interests: Online shopping, fashion, streetwear, lifestyle

---

### **6. Budget & Placements**

* Duration: 15 days
* Total Budget: ₹15,000
* Platforms: Instagram Feed, Reels, Facebook Feed & Stories

---

### **7. Campaign Performance (Estimated)**

* Reach: 1.5–2 lakh users
* Impressions: 3 lakh+
* CPM: ₹70–₹100

---

### **8. Conclusion**

The Meta Ads awareness campaign successfully positioned TrendHive in front of its target audience. The visually appealing creatives and precise targeting helped increase reach and engagement, building a strong base for future conversion-focused campaigns.

# **PROJECT REPORT 3**

## **Google Ads Campaign**

### **Brand:** SkillSpark

### **Project Type:** Performance Marketing Campaign

### **Platform:** Google Ads

---

### **1. Introduction**

SkillSpark is an online e-learning platform offering courses in digital marketing, coding, design, and business. Despite quality content, the platform faced challenges in generating traffic and enrollments.

---

### **2. Problem Statement**

* Low website traffic
* Low brand awareness
* High competition from Udemy and Coursera
* Low course enrollments

---

### **3. Campaign Objectives**

* Increase website traffic by 40%
* Generate quality leads
* Improve course enrollments
* Maintain CPC below ₹20

---

### **4. Campaign Setup**

* **Campaign Type:** Search Campaign
* **Ad Format:** Responsive Search Ads, Display Ads
* **Target Audience:** 18–30 years, NCR region

---

### **5. Keyword Strategy**

* High-intent keywords (e.g., “Best digital marketing course”)
* Long-tail keywords for better conversions

---

### **6. Budget & Bidding**

* Daily Budget: ₹1,000
* Duration: 30 days
* Bidding: Maximize Clicks → Maximize Conversions

---

### **7. Results Achieved**

* Clicks: 4,500
* Impressions: 1,20,000
* CTR: 3.75%
* Avg CPC: ₹18
* Leads Generated: 650
* Enrollments: 210
* ROI improved by 28%

---

### **8. Conclusion**

The Google Ads campaign delivered strong results by focusing on high-intent keywords and search ads. It significantly improved traffic, lead generation, and enrollments while maintaining cost efficiency.

# **PROJECT REPORT 4**

## **On-Page SEO Audit & Optimization**

### **Organization:** MyCaptain

### **Project Type:** On-Page SEO Optimization

---

### **1. Introduction**

MyCaptain is an online learning platform providing skill-based courses and mentorship. On-page SEO is crucial for improving visibility, rankings, and user experience. A detailed audit was conducted to optimize essential on-page elements.

---

### **2. Objectives**

* Improve search engine visibility
* Optimize meta tags, headings, and content
* Enhance user engagement
* Align content with search intent

---

### **3. Audit Tools Used**

* SEOptimer
* Manual content and UX analysis

---

### **4. Issues Identified**

* Non-optimized title tags and meta descriptions
* Weak H1 headings
* Poor keyword consistency
* Missing image ALT text
* Generic internal linking

---

### **5. Optimization Solutions**

* Optimized title tags and meta descriptions
* Added keyword-rich H1 and content (500–800 words)
* Improved internal linking with descriptive anchors
* Added image ALT text and schema markup

---

### **6. Expected Impact**

* Higher keyword rankings
* Improved organic traffic
* Better user engagement
* Stronger SEO foundation

---

### **7. Conclusion**

The on-page SEO audit showed that MyCaptain has strong design and offerings but lacked SEO optimization. Implementing the suggested improvements can significantly enhance organic visibility and attract more qualified learners.
